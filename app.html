App Development

Meetup

This web/mobile application streamlines the process for students to register via a Google Form. Administrators can initiate new rounds and automatically notify students via email. Students have the flexibility to opt in or out of each round through unique links, and administrators can efficiently match students based on their preferences, sending email notifications to the matched groups, facilitating a seamless and organized matching system.

Wasteagram

Wasteagram, a mobile app that enables restaurants/coffees employees to document daily food waste in the form of "posts" consisting of a photo, number of leftover items, the current date, and the location of the device when the post is created. The application will also display a list of all previous posts.

Journal

This Flutter mobile app, powered by SQFlite, provides users with a versatile journaling experience, allowing them to record entries with dates and ratings. When viewed in landscape mode, the app dynamically adapts to enhance readability and user interaction, ensuring a seamless and engaging journaling journey.

Call Me Maybe - Job App

This Flutter mobile app seamlessly combines the functionality of a digital business card and a resume builder, making networking and job application processes efficient. Additionally, it includes an interview predictor feature.

Vocabulary Builder

This Flutter-based mobile app is your go-to vocabulary builder, offering a daily dose of random words for expansion. You can also bookmark your favorite words for convenient revisits and accelerated language growth.
